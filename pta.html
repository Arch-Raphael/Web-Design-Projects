About Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta is a picturesque town located in the state of Kerala, India. Known for its lush green landscapes, temples, and rich cultural heritage, it is a popular destination for both pilgrims and nature enthusiasts. The town is situated on the banks of the River Achankovil and offers a unique blend of spirituality and natural beauty.

Popular Attractions

  • Sabarimala Temple: A famous pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Ayyappa.
  • Perunthenaruvi Waterfall: A beautiful waterfall surrounded by lush greenery.
  • Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple: A historic temple famous for its annual boat race.
  • Chettikulangara Bhagavathy Temple: A well-known temple and cultural hub.
